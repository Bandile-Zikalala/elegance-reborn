About Elegance Reborn

Our Story

Elegance Reborn is proudly a South African brand. Born in Soweto, made for the world. We work with local suppliers and celebrate African elegance in every stitch. When you wear Elegant Reborn, you support industry and talent. Elegance Reborn was established in 2026 by Bandile Zikalala, a young lady from deep in the rural areas of Kwa-Zulu Natal, inspired by the ways of living and how society perceives themselves there. She then had an idea to start a modest fashion barnd to cater to people from differnt backgrounds both locally and internationally. She believed she could bring back the spotlight to modest fashion and dressing.
